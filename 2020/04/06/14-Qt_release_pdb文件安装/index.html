Qt_release_pdb文件安装

  1. 打开MaintenanceTool.exe 选择 Add or remove components

  2. 设置->资料库档案-> 临时资料库档案添加一下地址
    https://download.qt.io/online/qtsdkrepository/windows_x86/desktop/qt5_5140/
    https://download.qt.io/online/qtsdkrepository/windows_x86/desktop/qt5_5140_src_doc_examples/
    https://download.qt.io/online/qtsdkrepository/windows_x86/desktop/tools_mingw/

  3. 点击下一步，添加、删除组件，选择安装调试信息。
